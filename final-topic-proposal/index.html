GIFs and memems to understand the younger generation

What the topic is about

Internet memes and GIFs are used to celebrate, mock or satirize current events. Nowadays these two forms of communication have become a defining part of how teenagers communicate in the digital world. Considering one big event (like Coronavirus or American Election) what can we understand about the younger generation? How did they use this tool? What insight about the society we have by analyzing it?

Who will be the audience?

Social media companies, geeks, young people, consulting companies

Existing similar project

In my week's research I haven't found any data project around how memes are used but a lot of very good articles. The only big project I found was about how memes spread around the web.

Starting from how memes are born to a study around the memes about the topic of world war III that has been around in the first days of 2020.
